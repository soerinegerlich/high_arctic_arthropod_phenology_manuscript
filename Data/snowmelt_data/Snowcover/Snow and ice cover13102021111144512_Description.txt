BioBasis Zackenberg - Abiotics - Snow and ice cover
Data on the snow and ice cover fraction in permanent plant and arthropod plots. For further details, please refer to the newest BioBasis manual available at the website.
DOI and permanent link: https://doi.org/10.17897/BKEX-JR94
